/ About

Quant rigor, business clarity.

My career began with a data analyst internship — my first taste of turning raw operational data into decisions a business could act on. I then moved into actuarial science, using mathematics, statistics, and financial theory to price risk and reserve for an uncertain future. That foundation in rigorous, defensible modeling now drives my work in business intelligence, where I design and lead the data systems a fast-moving FMCG business runs on.

In under two years, I've grown from that intern role to founding and leading an internal BI team — across four roles and three industries, from automotive and insurance to retail analytics. I turn scattered data into a single source of truth, then into the forecasts, dashboards, and decisions leaders act on with confidence. My focus never changes: pair rigorous analysis with clear communication, and own the outcome from first question to final decision.

Equal parts analyst, engineer, and strategist — I design the single source of truth that powers confident executive action.
